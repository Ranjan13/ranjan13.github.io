ABOUT

I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore under Dr. Erik Cambria and an A.I. Engineer at Naditech Social Robotics Group at Nova Hub, working with Prof. Nadia Thalmann. I have been working in the field of Natural Language Processing and Sentiment Analysis for 4 years and 2 years in Human-Robot Interaction. I am currently a part of core team at Naditech. Naditech is an early startup with a vision to build state-of-the-art social robots both in hardware and software. My role includes implementing software modules for natural language understanding, emotion recognition, working & long term memory. Being the part of core team, my responsibilities are not limited to software but also include brainstorming with the hardware team for building a robot that is compatible with the software platform.
